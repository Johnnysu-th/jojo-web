JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a long-running manga series by Japanese artist Hirohiko Araki, first serialized in 1987 and still ongoing. The story follows generations of the Joestar family through fantastical adventures, combining action, mystery, and supernatural elements. Known for its iconic art style, bold character designs, and the inventive “Stand” ability system, JoJo has significantly influenced manga culture and is celebrated as a groundbreaking fusion of art and pop aesthetics.

This website will introduce the plot and characters of first three parts of "JoJo's Bizarre Adventure" and its Special settings.
